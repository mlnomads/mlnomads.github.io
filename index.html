About MLNomads

MLNomads is a research community dedicated to building transparent, interpretable AI systems. We believe that the future of artificial intelligence must be understandable, auditable, and responsible.

Our work is rooted in physics-inspired approaches to neural networks, creating models where every decision can be traced, understood, and trusted. We call this white-box AI.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MLNomads?

MLNomads is an open research community focused on building responsible, transparent AI systems. We develop white-box models where every decision can be traced and understood.

What is "white-box AI"?

White-box AI refers to machine learning models that are fully interpretable and transparent. Unlike black-box models, every weight, activation, and decision can be inspected, understood, and audited.

How can I join the community?

Join our Discord server to connect with other members, participate in discussions, and find ways to contribute. All skill levels are welcome!

How can I contribute?

All our work is open source on GitHub. You can contribute code, documentation, research, or feedback. Check the issues tab on any repo to find tasks suited to your skills.

Do I need a PhD to participate?

Absolutely not! We welcome members of all experience levels — students, hobbyists, professionals, and researchers. Our community is about learning together and building in the open.

Where can I find upcoming events?

We host events on Luma including workshops, paper readings, and community calls. You can also check the Events section on this page.
