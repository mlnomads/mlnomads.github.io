About

MLNomads is a community of AI enthusiasts, researchers, and engineers. We gather around hackathons, workshops, paper readings, and meetups — and then we move on to the next thing.

No product to sell, no roadmap to ship. Just people learning in the open, sharing what they find, and showing up for each other. Whether you're getting started or deep in research, there's a spot at the fire for you.

Questions

What is MLNomads?

A community of AI enthusiasts, researchers, and engineers. We organize hackathons, workshops, paper readings, and meetups — online and in person.

Why "nomads"?

Because we don't sit in one place. We move between topics, between papers, between cities, between disciplines. Every event is a new camp.

How do I join?

Drop into the Discord, or come to an event. That's it. No application, no fee, no gatekeeping.

Do I need to be an expert?

No. Students, hobbyists, professionals, researchers — all welcome. The point is to learn together.

Where do you meet?

Events are listed on Luma. Some are online, some are in person. Subscribe there to get notified.

How can I help?

Show up. Give a talk. Host a session. Share what you're working on in Discord. That's how the caravan keeps moving.
