About MLNomads

MLNomads is a community of AI enthusiasts, researchers, and engineers who learn and build together. We organize hackathons, workshops, and events around transparent, interpretable AI.

Our focus is white-box AI — models where every decision can be traced and understood. Whether you're just getting started or deep in research, there's a place for you here.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MLNomads?

MLNomads is an AI community that organizes hackathons, workshops, and events around transparent, interpretable AI. We also build open-source tools together.

What is "white-box AI"?

White-box AI refers to machine learning models that are fully interpretable and transparent. Unlike black-box models, every weight, activation, and decision can be inspected, understood, and audited.

How can I join the community?

Join our Discord server to connect with other members, participate in discussions, and find ways to contribute. All skill levels are welcome!

How can I contribute?

Join our events, participate in hackathons, or contribute to our open-source projects on GitHub. You can also help with documentation, research, or just share what you learn.

Do I need a PhD to participate?

Absolutely not! We welcome members of all experience levels — students, hobbyists, professionals, and researchers. Our community is about learning together and building in the open.

Where can I find upcoming events?

We host events on Luma including workshops, paper readings, and community calls. You can also check the Events section on this page.
